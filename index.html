About & Academic Mission

Serving as an Associate Professor at ACE Engineering Academy and College alongside concurrent responsibilities as a Research Scholar at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, my work bridges the gap between infrastructure configuration modeling and next-generation cybersecurity parameters.

Over an distinguished academic tenure spanning more than 18 years, I have coordinated complex university laboratory frameworks, structured advanced cryptographic network architectures, and guided future computing professionals through advanced routing methodologies.
